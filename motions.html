The motions and the themes therein debated at Centrale's IV were:
Prelims:
1> THBT all trials should be settled by a jury and not a judge.
Should laws that require special training to be understood exist at all? Can a jury be truly democratic? (For Jury)
2> THW allow employers to offer a non-pregnancy clause to their female employees.
Illusionary choices offered to women in the workplace.
3> THBT governments should end direct international aid and donate all money to NGOs instead.
Direct international aid was defined as directly giving cash to a country.
Should development be bottom up, through local action in small communities, or top down, through big infrastructure projects?
4> THBT newspapers should remove the byline.
Can and should newspapers be objective?
5> THW move the residents of Les Banlieues to Central Paris
Context: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_situation_in_the_French_suburbs
Should integration be forced by physical proximity?
